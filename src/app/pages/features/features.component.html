Works with ADGA

No more copy/paste. What used to be hours of entering goats one by one, having to look up the same information each time, is now just the click of a button.

When editing your goats, a convenient Sync All button fetches all your owned goats, their registration info (such as name, id, etc.), and all the necessary goats ("related goats") to automatically construct a pedigree.

Personalized recommendations

Some things just aren't worth your time, like if your goats' barn names are always the last word in their registered name.

DigiGoat suggests those details for you so all you have to do is click the input box and it's filled in. The same goes for setting up your website for the first time—DigiGoat suggests copy so you don't have to wonder what to write.

Open Graph Protocol

Goat pages support the Open Graph Protocol so that when you share a goat's page on social media, it looks great and includes the right information—name, description, and pictures.

Optimized For Search Engines

DigiGoat is optimized for search engines so your site can rank higher in search results and help more people find your herd.

Pages are rendered in a way that search engines can easily understand, instead of the opaque blobs many website builders produce.

Analytics Support

DigiGoat comes with built-in support for Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity. This means that you can track how many people visit your website and what they do on it, from page views and traffic sources to heatmaps and session recordings.

IndexNow Support

DigiGoat supports IndexNow so that when you publish changes, DigiGoat automatically detects which pages are modified and notifies search engines, helping your site stay fresh in search results.

Works offline

The biggest downfall of many web-based editors is that they require an active internet connection. DigiGoat lets you work offline so you can add new photos and updates right away and publish when you're back on a reliable connection.

Internet Efficient

Unlike most website editors that require a constant, high-speed connection, DigiGoat only uses the internet when it's actually needed—such as syncing with ADGA or when you publish changes.

Open source

Every fragment of DigiGoat's code is fully public for inspection. You can rest easy knowing there's no hidden behavior behind your farm's website.

Community driven

New features are based on public request. If you have an idea that would make your farm website better, you can share it and help shape DigiGoat's roadmap.

Personalized support

Support comes from a real person who can remember your farm and setup, instead of a faceless call center. You don't have to play 20 questions every time you need a hand.

View live preview

You can view a live preview of your website as you edit it. This lets you see exactly what your site will look like before you publish, so you can catch layout or content issues early.

Live preview with GFM Markdown support

The live preview supports GitHub Flavored Markdown, so you can use Markdown to format text on your website and see the result in real time—even using an offline compiler that closely matches the online one.

Live preview works offline

The live preview itself works offline, so you can keep refining your content and layout even when you don't have an internet connection.

SPA

DigiGoat is a Single Page Application (SPA), which means your website feels fast and responsive. After the initial load, navigating around your site doesn't require full page reloads.

Optimized Image Loading

DigiGoat uses lazy loading so images are loaded only when they're needed, while still ensuring key goat photos appear immediately when a page is opened.

Automated SEO

DigiGoat includes automated SEO so your website is optimized for search engines by default, without you having to manage complex settings.

Ad-Free

DigiGoat is ad-free, so there are no distracting ads on your website—just your farm and your goats.

Custom Domain

DigiGoat includes a custom domain so you can have your own address, like www.yourfarm.com, instead of a generic subdomain.

Custom Emails

DigiGoat supports custom email addresses like info@yourfarm.com or support@yourfarm.com to match your domain and keep your farm communication professional.

Price

DigiGoat is competitively priced at $100/year. While it may be more than the entry-level plans on some builders, it includes many features that would otherwise be extra—especially when you factor in a custom domain.

Youth Discount

Recognizing that $100/year can be a lot for a 4-H project, DigiGoat offers a youth discount to make it more accessible.

100% Test Coverage

DigiGoat has 100% test coverage, meaning every part of the system is exercised by automated tests to help ensure reliability as the platform evolves.
